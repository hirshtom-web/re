Executive Summary

Gentle Woods is a newly developed 108-unit build-to-rent community located in Jacksonville, Florida. The project consists of twenty-seven four-unit residential buildings totaling more than 103,000 square feet.

Currently, 76 units have been completed and are approximately 95% occupied. The remaining 32 units will be completed under one of two available acquisition structures, providing flexibility for investors.

The community has qualified for Florida's Live Local tax abatement program, with certificates already issued and an anticipated 70% property tax reduction beginning in 2026.

Each building is individually platted, creating optional future disposition strategies by allowing buildings to be sold separately rather than only as a single portfolio.
