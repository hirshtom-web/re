Executive Summary

Acquire a 99,000-square-foot industrial warehouse leased to United Parcel Service, Inc. located at 2730 Kirby Cir NE in Palm Bay, Florida. The property provides investors with exposure to one of the world's largest logistics and shipping companies through a corporate-backed industrial investment.

The asset features a new five-year NNN lease structure with a fair market value renewal option, 3% annual rental increases, and minimal landlord responsibilities, providing investors with predictable income and long-term stability.

UPS represents one of the world's largest publicly traded logistics companies with approximately $88.7 billion in annual revenue and a longstanding operational presence across global markets.

The property benefits from a highly functional industrial layout featuring truck courts, loading infrastructure, drive-in bays, and efficient access to major transportation corridors including Interstate 95 and U.S. Highway 1.

Located in Florida's Space Coast region, Palm Bay benefits from strong population growth, expanding employment, and proximity to major aerospace and technology employers including SpaceX, Blue Origin, Boeing, Lockheed Martin, and L3 Harris.
