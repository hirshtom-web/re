Executive Summary

ARION Apartments is a 200-unit garden-style apartment community located in Tallahassee, Florida. Following the complete acquisition of all condominium units, the property was converted into a traditional multifamily investment and substantially renovated.

Over the past 18 months, ownership completed a comprehensive capital improvement program including new roofs, parking lot rehabilitation, code upgrades, plumbing and electrical improvements, pool renovations, and extensive interior unit renovations.

The property offers residents spacious one- to four-bedroom floorplans together with desirable amenities including a clubhouse, fitness center, swimming pool, playground, basketball court, tennis court, and professional on-site management.

Located less than four miles from Florida State University and minutes from Florida A&M University and Florida's State Capitol, the property benefits from one of the Southeast's most stable employment markets driven by government, higher education, and healthcare.
