Executive Summary

Anam Cara Lofts is a fully renovated 20-unit multifamily community located in the desirable North Beach neighborhood of Miami Beach. Originally constructed in 1945, the property underwent an extensive modernization program completed in 2022, creating a turnkey investment with modern interiors and upgraded building systems.

The property consists of two three-story buildings offering a mix of studios, one-bedroom apartments, and spacious three-bedroom residences. Renovations included new plumbing and electrical systems, stainless steel appliances, upgraded fire safety systems, landscaping improvements, and premium interior finishes.

Residents benefit from a walkable coastal lifestyle with immediate access to beaches, parks, restaurants, golf courses, and major retail destinations throughout Miami Beach and nearby Bal Harbour.

Current operations generate approximately $466,960 in annual NOI with additional upside through continued rent growth and pro forma income improvements.
