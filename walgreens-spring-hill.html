Executive Summary

Acquire a freestanding Walgreens pharmacy located within the Tampa MSA and leased on an absolute NNN basis, providing investors with a true passive income opportunity.

The property contains approximately 14,490 square feet on 1.14 acres and features a drive-thru pharmacy serving a well-established residential trade area.

Walgreens has more than eight years remaining on the current lease term together with six five-year renewal options, demonstrating long-term commitment to the location. The lease also provides scheduled 5% rent increases every five years, creating built-in income growth.

The property benefits from a highly visible hard-corner location adjacent to a Save A Lot anchored shopping center and surrounded by national retailers including Walmart Supercenter, Ross Dress for Less, Big Lots, McDonald's, Family Dollar, Dollar General, and 7-Eleven.

The surrounding five-mile trade area includes more than 89,000 residents and approximately 18,300 daytime employees, supporting strong long-term consumer demand for pharmacy services.
